Kaige Yan is currently an associate professor at college of communication engineering, Jilin University. He received his Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Houston in 2016. Before joining Jilin University, He worked at ARM Inc. located at Austin, Texas as a CPU design engineer. His research interests include computer architecture, VLSI, edge computing and deep learning processor design. More information can be found in his personal web page https://yankaige-jlu.github.io/.
