Jingweijia Tan is currently an associate professor at college of computer science and technology, Jilin University. She received her Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Houston, M.S. degree in Computer Science from University of Kansas, and B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Jilin University. Before joining Jilin University, she worked at Qualcomm Inc. as a Senior Engineer. Her research areas are computer architecture and high-performance computing (HPC), focusing on building reliable and energy-efficient computer systems from multiple disciplines (e.g., circuit, architecture, compiler, and application). More information can be found in her personal web page https://jingweijiatan.github.io/.
